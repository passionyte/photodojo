Photo Dojo JS
A project by Passionyte
How to Play

Photo Dojo JS is a fighting game based on the Nintendo DSiWare title "Photo Dojo" relased by Nintendo in 2009.
You can create your own fighter and background from images and sounds you provide!
It is simple to control your fighter! You can punch, jump, kick and shoot fireballs!
Outside of cool custom fighters and backgrounds, there is a Survival and a Versus mode.
In Survival, you fight hoards of your fighters and win once you defeat 100 of them! See how far you can get!
In Versus, you may fight a friend in two player multiplayer. Which one of you will come out on top?
